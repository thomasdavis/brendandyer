underscore micro templating

In the latest startup I have been working on, we decided to just use underscore.js templating as we reviewed benchmarks of the different templating engines and none of them could really be crowned king. Since then I have just learnt to use underscore.js micro templating engine as it works fine for us.

We place all our javascript templates which is basically 99% of our web app into an external file and host it on Amazon CDN. So our entire app is cached and distrubuted through edge locations which increases the speed the site is served and also saves us money on bandwidth.
